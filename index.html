What we are

Carbyn is named after carbyne — the hardest known form of carbon, harder than diamond. We operate as India's prototype composite lab — the studio large manufacturers won't be: flexible, fast, and built for small batches. Two divisions. One material. Zero compromises.

B2B · Engineering division
CARBYN LABS
Custom composite engineering for drone companies, EV startups, aerospace suppliers, and robotics firms. From a single prototype to a 200-unit batch — we handle briefs that large manufacturers refuse.
B2C · Enthusiast division
CARBYN PERFORMANCE
Premium carbon fiber parts for motorcycles, cars, and cycling — direct to enthusiasts. High-gloss weave, precision fitment, and the unmistakable material that serious builds demand.
